Natural History Museums and Biodiversity Science -- Integrative Biology (INTEGBI)C105[3units]
Description: (1) survey of museum resources, including strategies for accession, conservation, collecting and acquiring material, administration, and policies; (2) strategies for making collections digitally available (digitization, databasing, georeferencing, mapping); (3) tools and approaches for examining historical specimens (genomics, isotopes, ecology, morphology, etc); and (4) data integration and inference. The final third of the course will involve individual projects within a given museum. Also listed as Environ Sci, Policy, and Management C105.
