Spatial and Binocular Vision, Eye Movements, and Motion Perception -- Vision Science (VIS SCI)212F[2units]
Description: Introduction for graduate students to human spatial vision including contrast sensitivity, visual acuity, and spatial localization. Introduction to eye movements, motion perception, and motor and sensory aspects of binocular vision including pursuit, vergence, and saccadic eye movements, accommodation, stereopsis, and binocular space perception. Perception of real and apparent motion.
