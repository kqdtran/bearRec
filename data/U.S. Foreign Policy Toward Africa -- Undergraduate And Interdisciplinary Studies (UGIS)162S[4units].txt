U.S. Foreign Policy Toward Africa -- Undergraduate And Interdisciplinary Studies (UGIS)162S[4units]
Description: This course will focus on the evolution of U.S. foreign policy toward Africa from African independence until present day. Specific themes include economic development, China's economic expansion, foreign aid, democracy, and human rights. Specific attention will focus on the role of race and ethnic politics and their influence on U.S. policy.
