Health Care Technology Strategy -- Public Health (PB HLTH)222B[2units]
Description: This class will familarize the student with core principles and case examples of strategic decision-making related to the development, pricing, distribution and purchasing of biomedical technologies such as biopharmaceuticals and implantable medical devices. We will consider the perspectives of product developers/manufacturers, investors, and purchasers/users (e.g., insurers, hospitals).
