Principles of Conservation Biology -- Environmental Science, Policy And Management (ESPM)C103[4units]
Description: A survey of the principles and practices of conservation biology. Factors that affect the creation, destruction, and distribution of biological diversity at the level of the gene, species, and ecosystem are examined. Tools and management options derived from ecology and evolutionary biology that can recover or prevent the loss of biological diversity are explored. Also listed as Integrative Biology C156.
