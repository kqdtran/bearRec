Physicochemical Hydrodynamics -- Chemical And Biomolecular Engineering (CHM ENG)C268[3units]
Description: An introduction to the hydrodynamics of capillarity and wetting. Balance laws and short-range forces. Dimensionless numbers, scaling and lubrication approximation. Rayleigh instability. Marangoni effect. The moving contact line. Wetting and short-range forces. The dynamic contact angle. Dewetting. Coating flows. Effect of surfactants and electric fields. Wetting of rough or porous surfaces. Contact angles for evaporating systems. Also listed as Mechanical Engineering C268.
