Elementary Chinese -- Chinese (CHINESE)1A[5units]
Description: These courses are designed for students who are of non-Chinese origin and were not raised in a Chinese-speaking environment; or who are of Chinese origin but do not speak any dialect of Chinese and whose parents do not speak any dialect of Chinese. This series of courses provides elementary training in listening, speaking, reading, and writing in Modern Standard Chinese. It enables students to function adequately in Chinese-speaking places or communities.
