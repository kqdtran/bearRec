The Southern Border -- Ethnic Studies (ETH STD)159AC[4units]
Description: The southern border--from California to Florida--is the longest physical divide between the First and Third Worlds. This course will examine the border as a distinct landscape where North-South relations take on a specific spatial and cultural dimension, and as a region which has been the testing ground for such issues as free trade, immigration, and ethnic politics. Also listed as Education 186AC and Geography 159AC.
