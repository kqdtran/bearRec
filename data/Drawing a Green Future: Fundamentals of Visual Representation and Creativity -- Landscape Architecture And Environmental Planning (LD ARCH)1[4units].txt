Drawing a Green Future: Fundamentals of Visual Representation and Creativity -- Landscape Architecture And Environmental Planning (LD ARCH)1[4units]
Description: This introductory studio course is open to all undergraduate students in the University, who want to investigate the process of drawing as a method to learn how to perceive, observe and represent the environment.  This studio will encourage visual thinking as a formative tool for problem solving that provides a means to envision a sustainable future.  The focus will be on the critical coordination between hand, mind and idea.
