Introduction to Greco-Roman Magic -- Classics (CLASSIC)29[3units]
Description: Study of magical practices in the Greek and Roman worlds during the historical period (c. 750 BCE through 500 CE) as attested in literary, epigraphic, and papyrological sources. Attention is paid to the overall Mediterranean context and, in particular, Egyptian and Near Eastern influences on Greco-Roman traditions. Consideration is given to ways of analyzing and understanding magical practices, and the relationship between magic, religion, philosophy, and science.
