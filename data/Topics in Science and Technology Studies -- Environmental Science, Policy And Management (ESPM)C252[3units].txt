Topics in Science and Technology Studies -- Environmental Science, Policy And Management (ESPM)C252[3units]
Description: This course provides a strong foundation for graduate work in STS, a multidisciplinary field with a signature capacity to rethink the relationship among science, technology, and political and social life. From climate change to population genomics, access to medicines and the impact of new media, the problems of our time are simultaneously scientific and social, technological and political, ethical and economic. Also listed as Anthropology C254, Science and Technology Studies C200, and History C250.
