Credit Risk: Economic Concepts -- Master'S In Financial Engineering (MFE)230VA[1units]
Description: Introduction to credit risk modeling and conceptual overview of current techniques. Covers default probabilities, loss given default, correlation, credit portfolio analytics, bond valuation, loan valuation, and credit derivative valuation. Prepares students who are interested in a second course that will focus on model building. Students not interested in the technical details of modeling but who desire an understanding of how credit risk modeling is used in practice will benefit from taking this course.
