Polymer Science and Technology -- Chemistry (Department Of) (CHEM)C178[3units]
Description: An interdisciplinary course on the synthesis, characterization, and properties of polymer materials. Emphasis on the molecular origin of properties of polymeric materials and technological applications. Topics include single molecule properties, polymer mixtures and solutions, melts, glasses, elastomers, and crystals. Experiments in polymer synthesis, characterization, and physical properties. Also listed as Chemical & Biomolecular Engineering C178.
