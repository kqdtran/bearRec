Scandinavian Languages and Linguistics -- Scandinavian (SCANDIN)100A[4units]
Description: In the context of inter-Scandinavian communication, students will further develop their communicative competence, their reading and writing abilities and cultural understanding in their own target language (Danish, Norwegian, or Swedish). Workload: three hours of work outside class per week with one hour of individual work in the Berkeley Language Media Center. Oral and written midterm and final.
