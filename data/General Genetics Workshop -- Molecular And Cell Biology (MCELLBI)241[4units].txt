General Genetics Workshop -- Molecular And Cell Biology (MCELLBI)241[4units]
Description: This course is for Molecular and Cell Biology graduate students. It will teach in-depth introduction to genetics, including mechanisms of inheritance; gene transmission and recombination; transposable DNA elements; gene structure, function, and regulation; and developmental genetics. Some exams may be given in the evening. Courses 140 and 241 are taught concurrently. Students enrolled in 241 will also be required to participate in a one-hour specialized discussion section per week, led by the course instructor. This section will cover methodological background and will be based on the primary literature of the field.
