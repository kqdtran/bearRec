Social and Behavioral Health Research: Introduction to Survey Methods -- Public Health (PB HLTH)W219[3units]
Description: This course provides students with a thorough tool kit for designing survey questionnaires and for implementing telephone, face-to-face, mail, and internet surveys. The two three-hour, weekly class sessions are designed to convey practical knowledge with a case study approach used to complement the topical lectures. An SPSS laboratory also is given each semester.
