Introduction to Mechanical Systems for Mechatronics -- Mechanical Engineering (MEC ENG)102A[4units]
Description: Design, rapid prototyping, assembly, test, and evaluation of mechanical components and sub-systems used in mechatronic systems. Laboratory and mechatronic instrumentation. Design and optimization of beams and chassis. Two- and three-position synthesis of positioning mechanisms. Planar indexing via mechanical linkages. Rotary motion-conversion via cam-follower mechanisms. Crank-slider mechanisms for function generation. Permanent and temporary fasteners. Fabrication of actual prototypical devices.
