Digital TV and the World -- Journalism (JOURN)288[3,4units]
Description: Students not enrolled in the TV cycle who wish to learn essential techniques and examine new reporting forms are invited to apply for this experimental class. Students learn the basics of TV reporting, how to cover a slice of community life in America or abroad, and produce thoughtful works for distribution on the web and on the air. Students learn the rudiments of digital production, reporting, and editing. The course will emphasize solid reporting, clear expression, and original storytelling.
