The World According to Berkeley -- Letters And Science (LNS)110[1units]
Description: Exploration of various topics from the multitude of disciplines taught at UC Berkeley. Weekly guest lectures by prominent members of the UC Berkeley academic community. Topics will include groundbreaking research from the past, present, and future; UC Berkeley's unique contributions to society; and topics from speakers' areas of expertise. Learn about many of the remarkable fields of study UC Berkeley has to offer.
