Investing in Entrepreneurial Opportunities: Building an Investment Screen, Methodology, and Process -- Evening And Weekend Master'S In Business Administration (EWMBA)295G[2units]
Description: This course will provide students with an education in to the complexities and unique problems of entrepreneurship in companies with great growth potential, but that are facing significant challenges to achieving that potential. This class is designed to provide students with the tools and skills most critical to successfully screening, investing in, and/or leading companies that have both a great set future growth opportunities and a great set of current problems. This class will use case studies, practical valuation and other exercises, and the energy, enthusiasm, and intellectual capacity of its students to create a great learning environment.
