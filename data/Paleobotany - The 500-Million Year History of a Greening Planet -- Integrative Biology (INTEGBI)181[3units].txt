Paleobotany - The 500-Million Year History of a Greening Planet -- Integrative Biology (INTEGBI)181[3units]
Description: Introduction to evolution of plants and their ecosystems through time. Earliest plant life, transition to land, and the emergence of terrestrial ecosystems. Follow the evolution of major plant groups during important moments in time through the Phanerozoic (last 650 million years). Explore ancient fossilized plant communities, their ecological properties, and examine how major environmental upheavals affected their evolution. The profound impact plants have on the functioning of our planet's surface and atmosphere.
