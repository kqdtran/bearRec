Professional Preparation: Teaching in Nutritional Sciences -- Nutritional Science And Toxicology (NUSCTX)301[1-2units]
Description: Creative approaches to teaching nutrition to diverse audiences are emphasized. Participants will identify needs of target populations, formulate educational objectives, design and/or use motivational teaching strategies, and evaluate the impact of their teaching on knowledge, attitudes, and behavior. Undergraduates may teach nutrition to elementary school children. Graduates may become teaching assistants.
