Undergraduate Teaching of  Molecular and Cell Biology 32 Laboratory -- Molecular And Cell Biology (MCELLBI)180C[1-2units]
Description: Course consists of a weekly three-hour training session that focuses on laboratory techniques, instructional aids, and problem solving, plus an additional three-hour weekly laboratory where the UGSI is required to assist a GSI in the instruction of laboratory (answering questions, providing demonstrations, etc.). Students will be graded on lecture and laboratory attendance and preparation of one quiz.
