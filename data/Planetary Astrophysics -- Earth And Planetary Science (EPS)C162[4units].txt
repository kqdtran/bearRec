Planetary Astrophysics -- Earth And Planetary Science (EPS)C162[4units]
Description: Physics of planetary systems, both solar and extra-solar. Star and planet formation, radioactive dating, small-body dynamics and interaction of radiation with matter, tides, planetary interiors, atmospheres, and magnetospheres. High-quality oral presentations may be required in addition to problem sets and a final exam. Also listed as Astronomy C162.
