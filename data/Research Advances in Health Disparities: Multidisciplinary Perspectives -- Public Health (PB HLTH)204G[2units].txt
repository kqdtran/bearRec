Research Advances in Health Disparities: Multidisciplinary Perspectives -- Public Health (PB HLTH)204G[2units]
Description: A critical overview of major theories and research findings on health disparities from a multidisciplinary perspective. It will focus on applying major theoretical approaches from Public Health, Anthropology, Social Welfare, and other disciplines to understand and address health disparities. These approaches include social determinants of health, lifecourse perspectives, health as a human right, stress and bio-social perspectives, social contruction of disease, and healthcare access and quality.
