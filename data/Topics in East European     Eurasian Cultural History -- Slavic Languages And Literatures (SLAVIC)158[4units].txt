Topics in East European/Eurasian Cultural History -- Slavic Languages And Literatures (SLAVIC)158[4units]
Description: This course examines various dimensions of different East European and Eurasian (Central Asia, the Caucasus, Siberia) cultures (history, society, languages, literature, art). Variable topics. Instruction and readings in English; students with knowledge of the languages of the area are encouraged to do some reading in the original language.
