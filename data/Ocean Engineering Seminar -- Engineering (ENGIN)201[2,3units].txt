Ocean Engineering Seminar -- Engineering (ENGIN)201[2,3units]
Description: Lectures on new developments in ocean, arctic engineering. The optional third unit covers the analysis and design of arctic structures for ice structure interaction. The additional unit will require that students meet with the instructor one extra hour per week to work on an individual project. Topics covered: ice mechanics, determination of global and local forces, and other ice actions on structures. Term paper required. Sponsoring department: Engineering Interdisciplinary Studies.
