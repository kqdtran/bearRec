Internship in the Teaching of Literature/Linguistics -- Slavic Languages And Literatures (SLAVIC)310[1-2units]
Description: Weekly meetings with the instructor of the designated course. Discussion of course aims, syllabus preparation, lecture and assignment planning, grading, and related matters. Students may prepare a representative portion of the work for such a course (e.g., lecture outline and assignments for a course segment) and may participate in presentation of the material and in evaluation of samples of student work.
