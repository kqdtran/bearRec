Intermediate Sanskrit: Sastraic (Scientific) Sanskrit -- Sanskrit (SANSKR)101B[5units]
Description: Introduces students to Sanskrit sastra and related commentary.  Reading selections are generally taken from either the grammatical (vyakaraada), literary critical )alakarasatra) or the philosophical (darsana) tradition, including such works as Mahabhaya, Tarkasagraha, Kavyadarsa, etc.  Reading skills and familiarity with resources - hard copy and web-based - as well as current trends and scholarship in the relevant areas are emphasized.  Grammar is reviewed and explained as needed.  Students are expected to memorize at least one verse per class.  Emphasis is placed on correct prosody and pronunciation.  Submission of an annotated translation or similar project, assigned in class, is required.
