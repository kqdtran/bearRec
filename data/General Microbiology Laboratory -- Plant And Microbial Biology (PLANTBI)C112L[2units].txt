General Microbiology Laboratory -- Plant And Microbial Biology (PLANTBI)C112L[2units]
Description: Experimental techniques of microbiology designed to accompany the lecture in C112 and C148. The primary emphasis in the laboratory will be on the cultivation and physiological and genetic characterization of bacteria. Laboratory exercises will include the observation, enrichment, and isolation of bacteria from selected environments. Also listed as Molecular and Cell Biology C112L.
