Selected Topics in Comparative Perspectives and Area Studies in Sociology -- Sociology (SOCIOL)189[4units]
Description: Specialized topics in comparative perspectives and area studies in sociology that are not regularly offered in the curriculum may occasionally be offered under this number. The focus of the course will vary depending on the instructor in charge. One of the survey courses in comparative perspectives and area studies in sociology, 180C, 180E, 180I, or 180P, is recommended before taking this course.
