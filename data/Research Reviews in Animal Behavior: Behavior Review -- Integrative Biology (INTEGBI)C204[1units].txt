Research Reviews in Animal Behavior: Behavior Review -- Integrative Biology (INTEGBI)C204[1units]
Description: This course will discuss current publications in animal behavior. A student will summarize a paper and lead the discussion that follows. Occasionally, the group reviews a manuscript in preparation, or a thesis proposal. Not all participants need report, but all are expected to attend and enter into the discussions. Guest lecturers are invited each semester. Also listed as Environ Sci, Policy, and Management C204 and Psychology C204.
