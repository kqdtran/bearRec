U.S. Foreign Policy in the Middle East -- Undergraduate And Interdisciplinary Studies (UGIS)162M[4units]
Description: What are the United States' interests in the Middle East? Who and what determine those interests? And how are those interests pursued? This course addresses these questions in two parts. Students should leave this class with a strong understanding of the challenges that the U.S. faces in the Middle East, as well as an informed viewpoint regarding how well America is meeting those challenges.
