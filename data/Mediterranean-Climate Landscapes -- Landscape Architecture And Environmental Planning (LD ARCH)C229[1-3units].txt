Mediterranean-Climate Landscapes -- Landscape Architecture And Environmental Planning (LD ARCH)C229[1-3units]
Description: Comparative study of environmental conditions and human responses thereto in California and other Mediterranean-climate regions, with intensive treatment of a topic in environmental sciences, policy, planning, management, and/or landscape architecture, with application to California, Portugal, or other Mediterranean-climate regions. Students collect and analyze relevant data, synthesize, and complete technical reports, plans, and/or designs. Also listed as International and Area Studies C229.
