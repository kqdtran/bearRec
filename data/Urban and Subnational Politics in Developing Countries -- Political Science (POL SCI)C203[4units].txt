Urban and Subnational Politics in Developing Countries -- Political Science (POL SCI)C203[4units]
Description: This course will consider the political and institutional environment in which efforts to address metropolitan problems are developed, the financial and institutional vehicles used to provide services of different types, and the role of political parties and other forms of political organization in the development and allocation of services. Emphasis will be placed upon fertile areas for research within the social sciences. Also listed as Global Metropolitan Studies C203.
