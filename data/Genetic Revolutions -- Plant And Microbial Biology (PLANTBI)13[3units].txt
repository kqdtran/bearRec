Genetic Revolutions -- Plant And Microbial Biology (PLANTBI)13[3units]
Description: Genetic discoveries have changed our lives. All are controversial. Especially changed are human physical and mental health, agriculture, social systems, and worldviews. Having many DNA-sequenced genomes, including human, accelerates discovery. This course will study the science, history, and philosophical implications behind past discoveries and will contemplate future genetic revolutions.
