The Interdisciplinary Team: Improving the Care of Our Elders -- Health And Medical Sciences Program (HMEDSCI)215[2units]
Description: Through field experiences, readings, film, and discussions with multi-specialty healthcare professionals, students will gain an understanding of the purpose, function, and dynamics of the geriatric interdisciplinary team (GIT). We will compare and contrast the unique perspectives, values, and contributions of each profession, and students will consider how participation in a team will affect their own future professional practice.
