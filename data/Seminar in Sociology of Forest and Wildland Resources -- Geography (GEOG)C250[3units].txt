Seminar in Sociology of Forest and Wildland Resources -- Geography (GEOG)C250[3units]
Description: Individual projects and group discussions concerning social constraints to, and effects of, natural resource planning and management. Application of sociological theories to problems of managing wildland ecosystems. Students will examine topics of individual interest related to the management of wildland uses. Enrollment limited. Also listed as Environ Sci, Policy, and Management C255.
