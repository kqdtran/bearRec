Seminar in Tibetan Buddhism -- Tibetan (TIBETAN)C214[2,4units]
Description: This course provides a place for graduate-level seminars in Tibetan Buddhism that rely primarily on secondary sources and Tibetan texts in translation. Content will vary between semesters but will typically focus on a particular theme. Themes will be chosen according to student interests, with an eye toward introducing students to the breadth of available western scholarship on Tibet, from classics in the field to the latest publications. Also listed as Group in Buddhist Studies C214 and South Asian C214.
