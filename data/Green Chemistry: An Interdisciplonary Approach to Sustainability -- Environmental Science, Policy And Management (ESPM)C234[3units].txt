Green Chemistry: An Interdisciplonary Approach to Sustainability -- Environmental Science, Policy And Management (ESPM)C234[3units]
Description: Meeting the challenge of global sustainability will require interdisciplinary approaches to research and education, as well as the integration of this new knowledge into society, policymaking, and business. Green Chemistry is an intellectual framework created to meet these challenges and guide technological development. It encourages the design and production of safer and more sustainable chemicals and products. Also listed as Public Health C234 and Chemistry C234.
