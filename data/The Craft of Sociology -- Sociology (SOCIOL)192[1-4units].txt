The Craft of Sociology -- Sociology (SOCIOL)192[1-4units]
Description: This course provides an orientation to the study of sociology at Berkeley. In this class, students will gain exposure to the research and writing of Berkeley faculty, they will learn about the resources and opportunities available for their academic success and future goals, and they will develop their own sociological skills, notably around writing but also with regards to oral discussions and the presentation of numerical data.
