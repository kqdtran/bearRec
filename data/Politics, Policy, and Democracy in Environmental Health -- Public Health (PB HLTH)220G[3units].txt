Politics, Policy, and Democracy in Environmental Health -- Public Health (PB HLTH)220G[3units]
Description: This course provides an intensive introduction to environmental health policy in the United States and emphasizes the respective roles of science, policy, and politics in shaping environmental health protection. Students who complete this course will understand the basic tools for gaining policy leverage over environmental health problems and be able to critically assess the capacity of public institutions to address key environmental health issues.
