Nuclear Security: The Nexus Between Policy and Technology -- Public Policy (PUB POL)C285[4units]
Description: The course will review the origins and evolution of nuclear energy, how it has been applied for both peaceful and military purposes, and the current and prospective challenges it presents. The purpose of the course is to educate students on the policy roots and technological foundations of nuclear energy and nuclear weapons so they are positioned to make original contributions to the field in their scholarly and professional careers. Also listed as Nuclear Engineering C285.
