Cultural Representations of Sexualities: Queer Visual Culture -- Gender And Women'S Studies (GWS)C146[4units]
Description: This course examines modern visual cultures that construct ways of seeing diverse sexualities. Considering Western conventions of representation during the modern period, we will investigate film, television, and video. How and when do "normative" and "queer" sexualities become visually defined? Also listed as Lesbian Gay Bisexual Transgender St C146.
