Intermediate Tagalog -- Tagalog (TAGALG)100A[5units]
Description: The goal of this course is to enable students to increase their proficiency in Tagalog to at least the intermediate-high level of the national ACTFL Proficiency Guidelines. While speaking and listening comprehension will be stressed, training in reading and writing Tagalog will be an integral part of instruction. Films and video/audio materials will supplement written texts.
