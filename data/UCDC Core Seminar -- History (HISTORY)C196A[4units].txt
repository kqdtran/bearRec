UCDC Core Seminar -- History (HISTORY)C196A[4units]
Description: This course is the UCDC letter-graded core seminar for 4 units that complements the P/NP credited internship course UGIS C196B. Core seminars are designed to enhance the experience of and provide an intellectual framework for the student's internship. UCDC core seminars are taught in sections that cover various tracks such as the Congress, media, bureaucratic organizations and the Executive Branch, international relations, public policy and general un-themed original research. Also listed as Media Studies C196A, Political Science C196A, Sociology C196A, Political Economy C196A, History of Art C196A, Undergrad Interdisciplinary Studies C196A, and Gender and Women's Studies C196A.
