Seminar in Locomotion Energetics and Biomechanics -- Integrative Biology (INTEGBI)222[2units]
Description: Discussion and critique of scientific literature and current topics in the biomechanics and energetic cost of locomotion. Emphasis on terrestrial-legged locomotion. Topics include efficiency, musculoskeletal design, energy-saving mechanisms, muscle mechanics, gaits, effects of scaling, and comparative aspects.
