Privacy in the Digital Age -- Media Studies (MEDIAST)104D[4units]
Description: This course examines issues of privacy in contemporary society, with an emphasis on how privacy is affected by technological change. After an introduction to features of the American legal system and the theoretical underpinnings of privacy law, we will consider privacy in the context of law enforcement investigations, national security, government records and databases, newsgathering torts, commercial databases and First Ammendment limitations on privacy regulation.
