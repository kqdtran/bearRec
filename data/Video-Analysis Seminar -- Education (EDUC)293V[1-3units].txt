Video-Analysis Seminar -- Education (EDUC)293V[1-3units]
Description: This ongoing seminar is for anyone devoting a significant portion of a given semester to analyzing videotaped records as part of their research. Video-based data are now ubiquitous in educational research and this group is designed to help us all become more savvy at analyzing them. Strands of the seminar, each worth 1 unit of credit, are devoted to participating in video-analysis sessions, reading about video-analysis methods, and completing a paper on your own video-analysis project.
