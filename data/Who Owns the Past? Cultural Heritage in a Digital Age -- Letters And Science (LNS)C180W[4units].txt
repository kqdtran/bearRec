Who Owns the Past? Cultural Heritage in a Digital Age -- Letters And Science (LNS)C180W[4units]
Description: A cross-disciplinary exploration of cultural heritage on a global and local scale through discussion, debate, in-class activities, and team-based research projects that draw attention to the impacts of digital technology. Themes include the creation and management of heritage sites; the ethics of archaeologists as stewards of heritage; listening to multiple voices of interest groups; destruction and looting; and the preservation, conservation, and public presentation of heritage. Also listed as Anthropology C136K.
