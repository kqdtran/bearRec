Drugs and the Brain -- Molecular And Cell Biology (MCELLBI)C62[3units]
Description: The history, chemical nature, botanical origins, and effects on the human brain and behavior of drugs such as stimulants, depressants, psychedelics, analgesics, antidepressants, antipsychotics, steroids, and other psychoactive substances of both natural and synthetic origin. The necessary biological, chemical, and psychological background material for understanding the content of this course will be contained within the course itself. Also listed as Psychology C19 and Letters and Science C30T.
