Covering Asia -- Journalism (JOURN)235[1,2units]
Description: This course will look at selected countries of Asia from the inside out and the outside in, with perspectives, analysis, and guidance from commentators here and journalists there. Course will cover how to research and prepare for reporting; how to get to sources; how to assess risks in the field; how to define and develop the story. Students will follow international beats similar to those held by overseas correspondents and produce oral and written reports analyzing the coverage in various media.
