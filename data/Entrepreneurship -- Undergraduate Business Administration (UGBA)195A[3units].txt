Entrepreneurship -- Undergraduate Business Administration (UGBA)195A[3units]
Description: This course takes students through the new venture process using a 20-30 page business plan as the main deliverable. Students write a plan which lays a foundation for a new venture. Through the planning process that tightly links market and financcial planning, a business plan creates a set of standards to which investors and teammates can evaluate actual performance, laying the foundation for an "operation plan" once the business is launched.
