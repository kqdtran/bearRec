Directed Group Study for Freshmen and Sophomores -- Landscape Architecture And Environmental Planning (LD ARCH)98[1-4units]
Description: Supervised group studies of various topics relevant to department that are not covered in depth by other courses. Topics may be initiated by students. Open to students in good standing who, in consultation with a faculty sponsor, present a proposal with clearly formulated objectives and means of implementation. Intended for exceptional students. Topics vary from semester to semester.
