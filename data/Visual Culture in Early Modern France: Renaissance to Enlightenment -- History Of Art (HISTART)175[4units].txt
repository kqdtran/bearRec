Visual Culture in Early Modern France: Renaissance to Enlightenment -- History Of Art (HISTART)175[4units]
Description: French art from the 16th century to the mid 18th century. Beginning with the architecture, painting, prints, and decorative arts of the School of Fontainebleau, the course addresses the development of the Royal Academy and its interaction with popular culture, which culminates in the display of ambitious art in the public space of the pre-revolutionary Salon exhibition.
