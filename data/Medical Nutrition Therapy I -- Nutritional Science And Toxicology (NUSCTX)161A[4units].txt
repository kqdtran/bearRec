Medical Nutrition Therapy I -- Nutritional Science And Toxicology (NUSCTX)161A[4units]
Description: This is the first course of a two part series that addresses nutrition as a component of disease treatment. The Nutrition Care Process will be used a framework for nutritional status assessment, diagnosis and nutrition intervention and will be applied for all disease states in MNT I and II. Disease pathophysiology, diagnosis, medical and pharmacological treatments and nutritional therapies for prevention and treatment will be explored for a limited number of disease states in this course.
