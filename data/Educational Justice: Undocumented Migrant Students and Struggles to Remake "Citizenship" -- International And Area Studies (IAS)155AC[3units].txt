Educational Justice: Undocumented Migrant Students and Struggles to Remake "Citizenship" -- International And Area Studies (IAS)155AC[3units]
Description: This course focuses on undocumented migrant students in U.S. schools. It examines the political economy of migration and immigration, and the connection between immigration, poverty, and global displacement. It examines commonalities and differences among undocumented migrants from different ethnic communities based on race, racialization, and racism. It will analyze the ways in which race, citizenship, and "permanent foreignness" shape the undocumented experience in the U.S. school system.
