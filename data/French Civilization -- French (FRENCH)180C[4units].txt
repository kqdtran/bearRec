French Civilization -- French (FRENCH)180C[4units]
Description: Survey of French civilization: history, arts, and society, through the interpretation of literary texts. One course from 180A-180B-180C-180D is required for completion of the Option B French major. 180A: The Middle Ages; 180B: The Ancien Regime; 180C: The 19th Century; 180D: The 20th Century.
