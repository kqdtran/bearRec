The Science of Politics: Campaigns and Elections -- Undergraduate And Interdisciplinary Studies (UGIS)162O[4units]
Description: This class will teach you how to better understand the fundamental factors that drive elections in America and to learn some of the skills employed by political professionals. Many of your assignments will require you to apply the lessons of this class to real time events. Our goal in this class is to go beyond the spin and hyperbole of many election commentators and understand how voters decide and how strategists persuade.
