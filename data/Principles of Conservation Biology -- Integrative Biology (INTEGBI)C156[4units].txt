Principles of Conservation Biology -- Integrative Biology (INTEGBI)C156[4units]
Description: A survey of the principles and practices of conservation biology. Factors that affect the creation, destruction, and distribution of biological diversity at the level of the gene, species, and ecosystem are examined. Tools and management options derived from ecology and evolutionary biology that can recover or prevent the loss of biological diversity are explored. Also listed as Environ Sci, Policy, and Management C103.
