Chemical Biology III - Contemporary Topics in Chemical Biology -- Molecular And Cell Biology (MCELLBI)C212C[1units]
Description: This course will build on the principles discussed in Chemical Biology I and II. The focus will consist of case studies where rigorous chemical approaches have been brought to bear on biological questions. Potential subject areas will include signal transduction, photosynthesis, immunology, virology, and cancer. For each topic, the appropriate bioanalytical techniques will be emphasized. Also listed as Chemistry C271C.
