Chemistry of Soils -- Environmental Science, Policy And Management (ESPM)C128[3units]
Description: Chemical mechanisms of reactions controlling the fate and mobility of nutrients and pollutants in soils. Role of soil minerals and humus in geochemical pathways of nutrient biovailability and pollutant detoxification. Chemical modeling of nutrient and pollutant soil chemistry. Applications to soil acidity and salinity. Also listed as Civil and Environmental Engineering C116.
