Societal Risks and the Law -- Computer Science (Engineering) (COMPSCI)C79[3units]
Description: Defining, perceiving, quantifying and measuring risk; identifying risks and estimating their importance; determining whether laws and regulations can protect us from these risks; examining how well existing laws work and how they could be improved; evaluting costs and benefits. Applications may vary by term. This course cannot be used to complete engineering unit or technical elective requirements for students in the College of Engineering. Also listed as Political Science C79 and Statistics C79.
