Undergraduate Student Instructor for Integrative Biology Courses -- Integrative Biology (INTEGBI)194[1-3units]
Description: UGSI will work under supervision of instructor and/or GSI. The UGSI will attend any mandatory preparatory and review meetings, be available in the classroom (discussion or laboratory) to respond to student questions, facilitate lesson plans, perform other tasks as assigned. UGSIs do not evaluate students' work or assign grades.
