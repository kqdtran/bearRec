Individual Study for Doctoral Students -- Ethnic Studies Graduate Group (ETH GRP)602[2-8units]
Description: Individual study, in consultation with Group faculty, to prepare students for the doctoral oral examinations. A student will be permitted to accumulate a maximum of 16 units in 601 and 602, respectively, toward examination preparation. Units earned in these courses may not be used to meet academic residence or unit requirements for the master's or doctoral degree.
