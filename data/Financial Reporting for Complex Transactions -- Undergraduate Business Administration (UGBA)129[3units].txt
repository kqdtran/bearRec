Financial Reporting for Complex Transactions -- Undergraduate Business Administration (UGBA)129[3units]
Description: This course develops sophisticated users of financial information. Students will enhance their ability to understand the economic essence of important complex business transactions, focusing on topics related to major financial events in the lifecycle of an organization (IPOs, mergers and acquisitions, bankruptcies, etc.) Students' ability to identify and understand the financial reporting and tax issues related to these business dealings and accounting situations will dramatically increase.  Many fascinating transactions will be examined in an effort to understand the economic underpinnings of the transactions and their accounting representation in the financial statements.
