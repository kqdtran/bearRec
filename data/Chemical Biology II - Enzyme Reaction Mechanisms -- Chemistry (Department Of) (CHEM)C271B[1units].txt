Chemical Biology II - Enzyme Reaction Mechanisms -- Chemistry (Department Of) (CHEM)C271B[1units]
Description: This course will focus on the principles of enzyme catalysis. The course will begin with an introduction of the general concepts of enzyme catalysis which will be followed by detailed examples that will examine the chemistry behind the reactions and the three-dimensional structures that carry out the transformations. Also listed as Molecular and Cell Biology C212B.
