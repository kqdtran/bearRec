Leadership and Management -- Military Science (Army Rotc) (MIL SCI)141[3units]
Description: This course begins with a series of lessons designed to enable cadets to make informed career decisions as they prepare their accessions documents. The next lessons concentrate on Army operations and training management, communications and leadership skills, and support the beginning of the final transition from cadet to lieutenant. The course enables cadets to attain knowledge and proficiency in several critical areas that they will need to operate effectively as an Army officer, including the Army's training management system, coordinating activities with staff, and individual counseling skills. At the end of this semester, cadets should possess the fundamental skills, attributes, and abilities to operate as competent leaders and confidently shoulder the responsibilities entrusted to them.
