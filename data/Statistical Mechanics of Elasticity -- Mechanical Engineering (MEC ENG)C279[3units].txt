Statistical Mechanics of Elasticity -- Mechanical Engineering (MEC ENG)C279[3units]
Description: Introduction to statistical mechanics for engineers interested in the constitutive behavior of matter with a particular interest in continua. Systems of interest will be polymers and crystalline solids. Coverage includes introduction to statistical mechanics, ensembles, phase spaces, partitions functions, free energy, polymer chain statistics, polymer networks, harmonic and quasi-harmonic crystalline solids, limitations of classical methods and quantum mechanical influences. Also listed as Civil and Environmental Engineering C235.
