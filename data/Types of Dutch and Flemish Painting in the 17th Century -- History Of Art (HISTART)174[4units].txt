Types of Dutch and Flemish Painting in the 17th Century -- History Of Art (HISTART)174[4units]
Description: A general study of Netherlandish painting of the seventeenth century organized according to the genres or types of painting done at the time. The historical and social as well as the art historical contexts for the development in the Netherlands of such genres as history painting, portraiture, landscapes, still-life, and low-life and the kinds of meanings with which they were endowed.
