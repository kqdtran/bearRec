English Composition in Connection with Reading of World and French Literature -- Comparative Literature (COM LIT)R2A[5units]
Description: Expository writing done in connection with the reading of selected masterpieces of ancient and modern literature and the study of selected French texts read in the original. Course will prepare students for more advanced work in French. R2A satisfies the first half of the Reading and Composition requirement, and R2B satisfies the second half.
