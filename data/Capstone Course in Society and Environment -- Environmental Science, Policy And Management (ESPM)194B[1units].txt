Capstone Course in Society and Environment -- Environmental Science, Policy And Management (ESPM)194B[1units]
Description: Senior capstone project in the student's primary area of concentration and presentation to the ESPM Society and Environment faculty and majors. Required of all graduating seniors in the ESPM and Society and Environment major. Students who have completed ESPM 195, H196, or 197 may substitute that course for ESPM 194B.
