Sociology of Health and Medicine -- Public Health (PB HLTH)C155[4units]
Description: This course covers several topics, including distributive justice in health care, the organization and politics of the health system, the correlates of health (by race, sex, class, income), pandemics (e.g., AIDS, Avian Flu and other influenzas, etc.), and the experience of illness and interactions with doctors and the medical system. Also listed as Sociology C115.
