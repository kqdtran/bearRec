Comprehensive Health Education for Teachers -- Education (EDUC)W289[1units]
Description: This course addresses California's requirements for comprehensive school health education; finding and presenting reliable, trustworthy health information. Elementary teachers will focus on their responsibilities as primary health instructors. Secondary teachers will focus on the meaning and application of health education in their domain. Using a wiki online format the teachers will create one lesson plan, and a rubric for evaluating online health tools. This course is web-based.
