Topics in the History of Medicine and Public Health -- Public Health (PB HLTH)282[2,3units]
Description: A series of lectures and seminars providing detailed scrutiny of selected topics in the history of medicine, public health, and the allied health sciences. The precise content will vary from year to year and may reflect, in part, topics of class interest. Students electing to take the course for 3 units will be assigned a research topic.
