Language Spread -- Linguistics (LINGUIS)C139[3units]
Description: Linguistic background and the general principles of language spread. Mechanisms of language spread, including creolization-decreolization, language planning, and the role of bilingualism. Case studies in language spread, including Austronesian, Indo-European, Amerindian, Uralic, African, Sinitic, and Australian languages. Relationship of language spread to immigration and culture spreads. Also listed as Slavic Languages and Literatures C139.
