Migration and Health: A U.S.-Mexico Binational Perspective -- Public Health (PB HLTH)212C[2-3units]
Description: Building upon expertise on migration from Mexico to the U.S., the goal of this course is to strengthen students' knowledge and understanding of public health issues of immigrants and the effects that migration has on the health/disease issues of communities in the countries of origin, transit, and destination. Students will explore successful public health intervention programs targeting these populations.
