Web Architecture and Information Management -- School Of Information (INFO)153[3units]
Description: This course focuses on understanding the Web as an information system, and how to use it for information management for personal and shared information. The Web is an open and constantly evolving system which can make it hard to understand how the different parts of the landscape fit together. This course provides students with an overview of the Web as a whole, and how the individual parts fit together. It provides students with the understanding and skills to better navigate and use the landscape of Web information.
