Consciousness -- Psychology (PSYCH)222[3units]
Description: Survey of psychological, philosophical, and neuroscientific approaches to consciousness. Introspection. The mind-body problem. Automaticity. Explicit-implicit dissociations in memory, perception, and thought. Implicit emotion and motivation. Sleep and dreams. Anesthesia and coma. Hypnosis. Meditative states. Consciousness in nonhuman animals and computing machines.
