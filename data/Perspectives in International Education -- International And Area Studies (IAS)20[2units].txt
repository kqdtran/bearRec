Perspectives in International Education -- International And Area Studies (IAS)20[2units]
Description: This course will examine developments in the field of international education in light of the acceleration of the globalization of economies and the internationalization of cultural flows since the 1980s. In this context, the course will explore cross-cultural issues, economic trends, gender questions, and political considerations as they impinge upon international education programs. Particular attention will be given to the UC Education Abroad Program as a means of understanding the structure, scope, rationale, and characteristics of contemporary international education programs in the United States and corresponding institutions in Latin America, Africa, Asia, and Europe.
