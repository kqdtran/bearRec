Beginning Ballet Technique -- Theater, Dance, And Performance Studies (THEATER)147A[2units]
Description: This course is designed for contemporary/modern dancers interested in learning ballet vocabulary, technique, and alignment principles in order to support their contemporary/modern training. The course is intended to be taken in conjunction with one of the modern/contemporary dance technique courses offered by TDPS (40, 141, or 142).  Beginning level.  Audition first day of class.
